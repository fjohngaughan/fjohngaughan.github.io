About Me
While working as a legal secretary, I found myself drawn to the possibilty of automating tasks and seeking out software to improve our research and workflow. That curiosity, combined with my love for careful thinking, creativity, and innovation led me to programming. I'm driven to continually learn and use the technologies organizations need to create better solutions.

When I'm offline, you'll often find me in long conversations with family or friends, on a walk, reading about moral psychology and cognitive bias... or watching YouTube breakdowns of movies I've never seen.
